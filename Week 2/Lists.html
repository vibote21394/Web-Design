Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe fpr deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in abowl
  3. Once butter has melted add creams and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
